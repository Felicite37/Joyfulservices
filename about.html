About Joyful Services

Bringing professional culinary excellence to Kigali families

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Joyful Services started with a simple mission: to bring professional culinary expertise to every household in Kigali. What began as a small venture has grown into the most trusted personal chef and catering service in Rwanda.

Our team of certified chefs and culinary professionals are passionate about delivering not just meals, but experiences. We believe that great food brings joy, health, and connection to every table.

Today, we serve over 500 families and have catered to countless events, all while maintaining our commitment to quality, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.
